01 — Who I am

Building at the intersection
of AI & systems

I'm Yerramsetti Sai Mohith — a developer who finds joy in building things that are fast, smart, and purposeful. Currently working as a Jr. SDE at Chella Software, optimising performance-critical trading systems for India's leading stock exchange.

I hold a B.Tech in CSE from IIITDM Kancheepuram and a B.Sc in Data Science from IIT Madras — a dual-degree path that gave me engineering rigour and analytical depth in equal measure.

On weekends I benchmark small language models, build offline voice assistants, and explore the next AI integration rabbit hole. I'm also building Decoding Life — a podcast coming soon.
